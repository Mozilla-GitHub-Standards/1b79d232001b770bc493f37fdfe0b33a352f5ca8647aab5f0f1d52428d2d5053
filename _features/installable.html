--- title: Installable slug: installable description: Keep user's most useful app on their home screen without the hassle of an app store. usecases: - As a user, I want my favourite web applications (GMail, YouTube, Facebook…) to appear on my homescreen as if they had been installed from an app store. - As a browser developer, I want to offer users the possibility to have a native user experience of a web site. technologies: - web-manifest ---

There are a few web apps you use so much that you want them to be among your native apps, there on your homescreen. Not like a bookmark, because you don't want them to open in the browser, but as if they were real native apps. This is what you get with Progressive Web Apps.
